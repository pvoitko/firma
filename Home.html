About Us

A Few Words About Our Mining Company

Mining is an industry-leading mining company, focused on gold exploration and production worldwide. The company has five producing mines and two early stage exploration properties.

Founded in 1950 and publicly traded since 1955, Mining is headquartered in Atlanta, GE. We have approximately 24,680 employees and contractors, with the majority working at managed operations in the United States, Australia, Ghana, Peru, and Suriname.

Our company is a leader in value creation and the only precious metal producer listed in the S&P 500 index. Our purpose - to create value and improve lives through sustainable and responsible mining – is guided by five core values: safety, integrity, sustainability, inclusion, and responsibility. We are proud to have been named the mining industry leader in overall sustainability by the Dow Jones Sustainability World Index in 2015, 2016 and 2017.

Our philosophy is to operate mines safely by promoting a deeply-rooted value-based culture, leveraging mining skills developed over the company’s long history, and by innovating new practices.
